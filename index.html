What is HOLEP?

HOLEP is a minimally invasive surgical procedure to treat benign prostate disease. Benign prostate hypertrophy (BPH) is a common condition that affects most men over age 60. Medications are available to treat BPH and when men are ready to move on with their therapy, HOLEP is the least invasive treatment that CURES BPH.
