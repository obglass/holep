February 24, 2022: Building Your HoLEP Practice: Tips, Tricks & Technology Webinar


Watch Here: Webinar Link
TOPIC:

Holmium laser enucleation of the prostate (HoLEP) demonstrates superior efficacy and durability compared to other transurethral procedures and has become the standard of care for BPH treatments thanks to technological innovations.


On this webinar, our experts explore tips and tricks for building a new HoLEP practice, conquering the learning curve, and the importance of team empowerment.


Moreover, the webinar summarizes the difference between self-taught HoLEP versus existing mentoring programs and discusses leveraging new technologies such as the MOSES technology for achieving versatility and efficiency in the treatment of both soft tissues and stones.


Moderator:

Tim Large, MD


Presenters:

Chris Manakas, MD

Dan Gralnek, MD
